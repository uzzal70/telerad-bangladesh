our mission

CARES aims to function as a catalyst to researches in natural and social sciences in Bangladesh and channelling the researched information to the entrepreneurs who will require them to improve their services and products offered to people for improving the quality of life.

our vission

CARES has been envisioned as a virtual learning centre that will operationally incorporate individual researchers, institutions and programs and above all the next generation of people and technology. The fruits of research works shall facilitate real life activities beneficial to liberal market mechanism to reach all the strata of the population.
